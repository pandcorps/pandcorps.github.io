Fur-Guardians™ for Android™ and Java™

Classic platform gameplay with retro graphics

Fur-Guardians is a traditional platformer game.
It features 16-bit style graphics and old-school music and sound effects.
It's free to download and play with no in-app purchases and no ads.

Create your own furry character and defend the land of Fur-Gardia.
Run and jump through different environments to collect gems and defeat monsters from the Realm of Chaos.
